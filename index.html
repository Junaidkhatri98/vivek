About Magician Prince Vivek

Magic is not just an illusion it's a doorway to imagination.

With over 15 years of mesmerizing performances, Magician Prince Vivek is a master of stage illusions, close-up magic, and mentalism. A three-time National Award winner and Guinness World Record holder, he blends mystery, charm, and pure showmanship to create unforgettable experiences. His talent has captivated prestigious corporate audiences including Adani, Volkswagen, Welspun, Tata Chemicals, Agrocel, Orpat, Oppo, and Hindustan Unilever.
